## Introduction to Waste-to-Energy (WTE) Applications

### WTE (Waste to energy) using heat exchangers

This paper describes a WTE model utilizing a heat exchanger. 
First, this model operates two sets of incinerators, each representing a set of two units, 
depending on the desired power generation capacity.
